Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Open Utter → Settings → Subscription, then tap "Manage Subscription" or "Restore purchases". Sign in with the Apple ID you used to purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

On iPhone/iPad: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Utter → Cancel Subscription. You can also manage subscriptions from the App Store.

I'm having trouble with voice recording

Make sure you've granted microphone permission to Utter. If recording fails, try closing and reopening the app, or restarting your device. Check that you have a stable internet connection for transcription.

How do I delete my account and data?

Visit our Request account & data deletion page for instructions.
